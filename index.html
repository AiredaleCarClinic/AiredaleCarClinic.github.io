🏪 More than just your local garage 
Airedale Car Clinic is a Community Social Enterprise that offers service and MOT for all makes of cars. It is located at The Active Centre, Stansfield Road, Airedale, the same place as Airedale Computers . All the profits from Airedale Car Clinic go to Chrysalis Youth and Community Project.
Social Enterprise at it's best
🔧 Servicing and Repairs for all makes
At our garage, we offer a wide range of services, including MOTs, general repairs, and diagnostics. We provide excellent value for money and can service any make of car. As members of the National Service Network and 1-Link, we also offer high-quality service to leasing companies and fleet operators. You can enjoy our comfortable and warm waiting/viewing area, where you can use a computer with internet access while you wait.
Amazing value for money
⚙️ Clutches, Brakes, Exhausts, Wheel Balancing
Our workshop is modern and fully equipped, and our technicians are fully trained. We can handle most servicing and repairs, such as regular servicing and maintenance, brakes, clutches, exhausts, tyres and repairs, wheel alignment, wheel balancing, MOT testing, and general repairs.
