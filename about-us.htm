About us | Airedale Car Clinic
 

Airedale Car Clinic is more than just a garage. We are a community social enterprise that offers a wide range of vehicle services, from MOTs and repairs to tyres and exhausts, for all makes and models of cars. Whether you need a routine check-up or a major overhaul, our team of qualified and experienced mechanics will provide you with high-quality workmanship and friendly customer service.

Airedale Car Clinic is also a charitable organisation that supports the local community. We are owned and operated by Chrysalis Youth and Community Project, a registered charity that aims to improve the lives of young people and families in the Airedale area. All our profits are reinvested back into the charity, enabling us to fund various projects and activities that promote education, health, and social inclusion.

By choosing Airedale Car Clinic, you are not only getting a reliable and affordable car service, but you are also making a positive difference in your community. We are proud to be an award-winning social enterprise that has been recognised by the Social Enterprise UK Awardsand the West Yorkshire Social Enterprise Network. We are also accredited by the Good Garage Scheme and the Motor Industry Code of Practice, ensuring that we adhere to the highest standards of quality and ethics.

So, if you are looking for a car service that cares about you and your community, look no further than Airedale Car Clinic. Contact us today to book an appointment or get a free quote. You can call us on 01977 736281, email us at info@airedalecarclinic.co.uk, or check us out on Yell.
We look forward to hearing from you and serving your car needs.
